Help - Travel Booking System

Getting Started

Welcome to the Travel Booking System! To begin your journey:

  1. Create an account by clicking on the "Sign Up" link.
  2. Log in to your account.
  3. Explore available packages and select your desired destination.
  4. Proceed to book your travel package by following the prompts.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking within 24 hours of purchase for a full refund. After that, our cancellation policy applies.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact customer support via the contact form on our website or by emailing support@example.com.
